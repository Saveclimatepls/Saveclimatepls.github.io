🌍Climate Change Versus Global Warming🌍

Climate Change

  • 🌡️Changes in measurement of temperature, wind, and precipitation over a long period of time.
  • 🌡️This process is NOT safe for the earth

    • Global Warming

  • 🌡️Natural process of heating up the earth by greenhouse gases.
  • 🌡️This process is safe for the earth!
